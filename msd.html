Peer Park

Mobile Apps, Peer Economy, and Value Co-Production

Team: Three CMU Human-Computer Interaction Students
Timeframe: Four weeks
Methods: Ecosystem Collection Map, Storyboarding, Personas, Guerrilla Research, Wireframing
Tools: Sketch, Keynote, Adobe Photoshop

Summary

Our team was tasked with designing the User Experience for a peer economy mobile app. We had to select from one of four target areas. We selected homeowners with parkings spaces and drivers with limited parking options. Using the mobile platform we created PeerPark, a system that would bring two different user roles, with different goals, together in value co-production.

Ecosystem Collection

The first step was figuring out what we knew about the space we were working in and the assumptions that we were making

We concluded that we need to do more research to find out if homeowners feel comfortable lending out their parking space.
